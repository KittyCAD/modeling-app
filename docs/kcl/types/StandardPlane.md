One of the standard planes.




**This schema accepts exactly one of the following:**

The XY plane.


**enum:** `XY`






----
The opposite side of the XY plane.


**enum:** `-XY`






----
The XZ plane.


**enum:** `XZ`






----
The opposite side of the XZ plane.


**enum:** `-XZ`






----
The YZ plane.


**enum:** `YZ`






----
The opposite side of the YZ plane.


**enum:** `-YZ`






----
