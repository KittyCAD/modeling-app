**This schema accepts exactly one of the following:**

Add two numbers.

**enum:** `+`








----
Subtract two numbers.

**enum:** `-`








----
Multiply two numbers.

**enum:** `*`








----
Divide two numbers.

**enum:** `/`








----
Modulo two numbers.

**enum:** `%`








----
Raise a number to a power.

**enum:** `^`








----
